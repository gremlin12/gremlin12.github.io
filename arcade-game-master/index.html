Revenge of the Mutant Ladybugs

How to Play

GAME OVER

The Human Race is Doomed...

Giant ladybugs, mutated and energized by radioactive crystals, have overrun the city.

Only You Can Save Us

Get across the road to the water without becoming ladybug-food! On your way, collect as many crystals or other tokens as you can.

Use arrow keys to move your avatar.

Collect crystals to gain extra points.

Collect hearts to win extra lives.

Keys to the Secret Laboratory help control the enemy bug population.

Gain megapoints by collecting stars.
